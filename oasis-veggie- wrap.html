Oasis Veggie Wrap

Mage

Prep Time : 5 Cook Time : 5 Total Time : 10 Difficulty : Easy Servings : 5

Ingredients

1 15oz can lentils or chickpeas

1 cup shredded carrots

1 cup cucumber, diced

1/2 red bell pepper, sliced

wraps or tortillas

2 TBSP vegan mayo

1/4 tsp salt

1/4 tsp black pepper

Dressing:

1/4 cup Dijon mustard

1.5 TBSP agave nectar or maple syrup

1 TBSP oil

1/2 TBSP apple cider vinegar

1/4 tsp salt

1/8 tsp garlic powder

1/4 tsp ras al hanout

Directions

In the deserts of the Alyablah Caliphate, the portability of food is paramount. The hardy desert nomads prepare food for several days at a time - and generally prefer food that can be eaten on the go. This wrap delivers all of that and then some - even incorporating some of the traditional desert spices into the mix. Cool off with some cucumber while you relax at the next oasis - or eat this bad boy on the go. It's up to you.

Tip: Ras al Hanout is a North African spice mix that is sold at most grocery stores. Try your local organic market or order it online if you can't find it. One bottle will last you awhile!

Drain and rinse the canned lentils or chickpeas, then add to a large mixing bowl. Mash them up a bit with a large spoon or potato masher. You don't have to mash them into a paste, but a nice half and half ratio of chunks to smooth is the target.

Add 1/4 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp black pepper to the mashed lentils/chickpeas and mix it in thoroughly.

Shred your carrots, if needed. It's probably easier and not much more expensive to just buy pre-shredded carrots here. Dice the cucumber - then slice the red pepper into thin strips.

To make the sauce, toss all of the ingredients into a bowl or measuring cup and whisk until smooth.

Take a wrap and lay it flat on a plate. Spread a small circle of vegan mayo across the center. Then, smear a layer of the lentils/chickpeas as the base. Next. layer your raw veggies on top of the lentil/chickpea mixture. Be careful not to overfill the wrap. Drizzle some of the dressing on top.

Roll up the wrap and tuck in the ends so that the contents don't spill all over your lap or the floor.
