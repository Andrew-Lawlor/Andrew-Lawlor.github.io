Pickled Dragon Heart Pasta

★★★★★

Warrior

Prep Time : 5 Cook Time : 25 Total Time : 30 Difficulty : Easy Servings : 6

Ingredients

1 zucchini, chopped

1 1/2 cups frozen corn

3 cloves Garlic, minced

1 box chickpea rotini pasta (~9oz)

1 15oz can black beans

1 1/4 cups marinara sauce

1 15oz can fire-roasted diced tomatoes

1 4oz can diced jalapenos


Spices:

1/2 tsp dried oregano

1 tsp smoked paprika

1/4 tsp black pepper

1/4 tsp salt

fresh basil for garnish

Directions

Warrior tradition has long held that a Warrior must consume the heart of a dragon before coming into to his or her own. Many fail in this test, as dragon hearts are notoriously spicy. Thus, today we will target a milder young dragon. Your quest is to acquire a small can of pickled young dragon heart, and incorporate it into a simple pasta dish. If you best this challenge, you - and your taste buds - will be ready to adventure as a warrior.
Boil a pot of water and cook the rotini pasta according to package instructions. When the pasta is done, drain it and return it to the stock pot and set aside.
While the pasta is cooking, heat 1 TBSP of oil OR 2 TBSP of vegetable broth in a large skillet. Saute the zucchini and garlic over medium heat for 3 minutes. Next, add the corn, oregano, smoked paprika, salt and pepper and continue cooking for 3-4 minutes.
Drain and rinse the black beans, then add the drained beans to the skillet. Next, add the tomatoes (and their juices), and jalapenos to skillet. Stir and mix well.
Pour the marinara sauce into the skillet, stir and cook for a few more minutes on medium heat.
Finally, add the cooked and drained pasta to the skillet and stir thoroughly.
Serve, and and become the Warrior you were born to be.
