Orcish Fried Cabbage

Ranger

Prep Time : 5 Cook Time : 10 Total Time : 15 Difficulty : Easy Servings : 4

Ingredients

1 pkg vegan bacon (tempeh bacon or seitan bacon)

1 green bell pepper, chopped

1 medium onion, chopped

1/2 medium head green cabbage, chopped

1 clove garlic, minced

1 tsp garlic powder

1 tsp chili powder

1 tsp smoked paprika

1/2 tsp black pepper

1/4 tsp salt

Directions

The Orcs of the Zulunksu Tribal Lands are not renowned for their cuisine. Rangers from across the realms know the truth, however. Orcish cuisine is subtle, surprisingly refined and makes extensive use of green vegetables. Take Orcish Fried Cabbage, for example. It elevates the lowly cabbage to divine heights - combining sweet and smoky spices with subtle aromatics to create a delicious and nutritious result.
Chop the onion and pepper. Rinse the cabbage and roughly chop. Slice the vegan bacon into 1 inch pieces. Set aside.
Sauté onion and pepper for 5 mins over medium heat. Add garlic and dry spices and stir thoroughly. Sauté for 30 seconds. Add vegan bacon. Sauté for 2 more minutes. Add cabbage and mix thoroughly. Cook down cabbage for 6-7 minutes, stirring constantly. Add water 1 TBSP at a time if spices begin sticking to the bottom.
Serve and garnish with fresh parsley, if desired.
