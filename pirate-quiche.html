Pirate's Quiche

Prep Time : 5 Cook Time : 40 Total Time : 45 Difficulty : Easy Servings : 5

Ingredients

1 pkg Just Egg

1 10z bag Beyond Meat Crumbles

1 cup frozen potato hash

1 red bell pepper, diced

1/2 medium onion, diced

1 cup vegan cheese shreds

Spices:

1.5 tsp smoked paprika

1.5 tsp garlic powder

1 tsp black pepper

1 tsp red chili powder

1/4 tsp salt

Directions

This quiche is a real treasure - just the right amounts of meaty, cheesy and eggy. Just Egg is a fantastic egg substitute that provides body and substance to this quiche - and holds everything together. Impress and deceive your crew with this plant-based take on a classic quiche recipe.
Dice onion and pepper, set aside. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
Heat 1 TBSP of oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add onion and cook for 2 minutes.
Add red pepper and continue cooking for 3 minutes.
Add Beyond Meat Crumbles to the pan. Cook for ~minutes or so, until thawed. Once thawed, add your spices and stir well.
Next, add the frozen potato hash. Continue cooking until thawed.
Now, oil (or use a spray oil) a small casserole dish. Then, pour the contents of your skillet into the casserole dish. Pour the Just Egg product on top, making sure to distribute evenly.
Cook for 15 minutes in the oven at 400 degrees F.
Remove from oven - top with vegan cheese and cook for another 15 minutes at 400 degrees F.
Serve to your crew!
