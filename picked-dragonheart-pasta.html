Pickled Dragon Heart Pasta Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook time: 25 minutes Yield: 6 servings Ingredients: - 1 zucchini, chopped - 1 1/2 cups frozen corn - 2 cloves Garlic, minced - 1 package rotini pasta - 1 15oz can black beans - 1 jar marinara sauce - 1 15oz can fire-roasted diced tomatoes - 1 can diced jalapenos - 1/2 tsp dried oregano - 1 tsp smoked paprika - fresh basil for garnish
