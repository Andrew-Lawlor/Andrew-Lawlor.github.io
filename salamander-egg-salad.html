Salamander Egg Salad

Mage

Prep Time : 15 Cook Time : 10 Total Time : 25 Difficulty : Easy Servings : 6

Ingredients

2 15oz cans chickpeas

3 stalks celery, diced

3/4 cup seedless grapes, halved

1/2 cup pecans. chopped

1/2 cup onion, diced

1/2 tsp salt

1/2 tsp black pepper

1/3 cup vegan mayo

2-3 TBSP apple cider vinegar

1 TBSP maple syrup

Directions

Salamander eggs are prized by the elves for their sweet taste. In fact, they constantly make incursions into the Alyablah Caliphate to acquire them. When combined with chickpeas and a creamy yet tangy dressing, Salamander egg steal the show and seal the deal. Enjoy this light but protein-packed salad on toasted bread to make a sandwich.

Drain and rinse the chickpeas, then place them into a large mixing bowl. Mash them with a large spoon or potato masher until they are no whole chickpeas remaining. You don't have to mash them into a paste - find the middle-ground here!

Next, dice the celery and onion, cut the grapes in half, and then chop the pecans. Add them all to the bowl with the chickpeas. Now, add the vegan mayo, apple cider vinegar, maple syrup, salt and pepper. Mix everything thoroughly with a spoon. That's it!
