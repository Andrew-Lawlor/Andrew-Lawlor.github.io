Cerberus Dogs

Rogue

Prep Time : 5 Cook Time : 20 Total Time : 25 Difficulty : Easy Servings : 6

Ingredients

6 carrots, peeled

hot dog buns

1 cup vegetable broth

1/4 cup apple cider vinegar

2 TBSP tamari or soy sauce

2 TBSP Dijon mustard

1 tsp smoked paprika

1 tsp liquid smoke

1/2 tsp garlic powder

1/2 tsp onion powder

Directions

According to the Ronescan poet Dante, the mythical Cerberus is said to watch over the gluttons in the nine hells - punishing them for a lifetime of overindulgence. The mighty Cerberus would likely sit idly by even if you ate all six of these delicious carrot dogs - that's how nutritious and guilt-free they are! Beguile your friends and family at your next barbeque by grilling up some Cerberus dogs.

In a mixing bowl, combine the vegetable broth, apple cider vinegar, tamari or soy sauce, Dijon mustard, liquid smoke and spices. Whisk until somewhat smooth.

Peel the carrots, then place them in a shallow/flat dish, such as a small casserole dish. Pour the marinade on top of them. Cover, and marinate for 30 minutes to several hours.

Pour the carrots AND marinade into a stock pot. You want to make sure that there is enough liquid to completely submerge the carrots, so you may need to add 1 or more cups of water. Once you've done that, bring the pot to a boil. Boil for 20 minutes, covered.

Remove from heat and serve with traditional hot dog condiments, such as ketchup, mustard and relish. You can also grill the carrots for a few minutes per side after boiling - this will add a nice finishing touch, but is not necessary.
