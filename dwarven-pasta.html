Dwarven Pasta

Warrior

Prep Time : 5 Cook Time : 10 Total Time : 15 Difficulty : Easy Servings : 6

Ingredients

12 oz whole grain rotini pasta

1 15oz can fire roasted diced tomatoes

1 15oz can black beans

1 red bell pepper, chopped

1/2 medium yellow onion, chopped

1 4oz can diced green chilis

1 cup shredded vegan mozzarela

1/4 cup nutritional yeast

1 TBSP low-sodium Tamari or soy sauce

Spices:

1 TBSP chili powder

1/2 tsp ground cumin

1/2 tsp ground coriander

3/4 tsp garlic powder

1 tsp smoked paprika

Directions

Pasta was invented in the Ronescan Republic centuries ago - but has since spread across most of the realms. The Dwarves of the mountain duchies have an interesting take on it - combining short, stubby rotini pasta with their stout and smoky traditional spices. The result is astonishingly simple yet delicious.

Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. While the pasta is cooking, chop the onion and bell pepper and drain and rinse the black beans. Set aside.

Heat 1 TBSP of oil over medium heat in a large skillet. Sauté the onion for 2 minutes, then add the garlic and bell pepper. Cook for an additional 3 minutes.

Next, add the diced tomatoes, drained black beans, diced green chilis, nutritional yeast, Tamari or soy sauce and all spices. Stir thoroughly and cook for another 5 minutes. Stir occasionally so you don't burn the tomatoes.

Once the pasta is done, drain it and then add to the skillet. Then, add 1 cup of shredded vegan mozzarella and mix it all together. Continue cooking on medium/low until the cheese melts. Serve and enjoy.
